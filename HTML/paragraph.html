Paragraph Tag

Paragraph tags are used to define paragraphs in HTML documents. They are defined with the <p> tag.

Paragraphs are block-level elements, which means they will start on a new line and take up the full width of the parent element.

Paragraphs are commonly used to group text content together and provide structure to the document.
